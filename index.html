STAYING SAFE ONLINE

Workshops about online security for individuals, businesses, schools and parents.

About the workshops

Cyber attacks against us, our businesses but also our children are happening daily, and this trend is growing. In this day and age, you just cannot ignore online security. There are a few things you absolutely need to know, so don't wait until you get hacked to learn about it!

We are conducting online security workshops for individuals and businesses but also for schools and parents. The workshops are very accessible and are designed for people with no knowledge of online security whatsoever.

Please drop us a line at info@stayingsafe.online to register your interest.
