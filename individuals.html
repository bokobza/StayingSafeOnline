About the workshops

The workshops are currently taking place in Hendon (NW4) and are aimed at people who don't have any knowledge of online security and want to learn more.

We aim to provide you with all the tools you need to avoid having your data hacked, exposed or stolen. With the right knowledge and the right mindset, you can protect yourself easily and effectively.

From how to choose a password to how to edit your privacy settings on social media, these workshops will give you all the tools you need to become security savvy.

Features

Passwords

Most people use the same password for most of the websites they visit. In our workshops you'll learn why this is a terrible idea and what you can do about it. 

Social Media

We love to share, like, follow and be followed, but a lot of identity theft starts on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram. We'll teach you how you can do simple changes in order to retain your privacy and not be worried about being impersonated.

Healthy Habits

Simple changes can sometimes make a big difference. Learn how to be ready for any eventuality with some simple rules to follow that won't make you waste any time and will protect you for the years to come.

Malware and Trojans

How to avoid malware, trojans and worms? With more and more people being ransomed for their data, you absolutely need to know how to avoid them and what to do if you do get infected.
